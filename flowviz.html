Plane Controls

   


How do you use this Visualisation ?

  1. You can drag either the mouse or your touchpad to rotate the cylinder along its respective axes
  2. To zoom the cylinder you can use the mouse scroll feature
  3. Also you can use the Left / Right Arrow keys to move the cylinder along the canvas svg
  4. Use the plane controls to move the plan along the Z axis (it might take a while especially in the middle of the cylinder where points are denser)
